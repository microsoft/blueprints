Logo

A logo is a graphic mark, emblem, or symbol used to aid and promote public identification and recognition. It represents a company or a product.

Anatomy

  1. 1
    SizeBy default, Logo fills its container. There are also 3 fixed sizes: small, medium, and large.
  2. 2
    LabelThe label is the name of the logo. It is optional and can be used to provide more context to the logo for screen-readers.
